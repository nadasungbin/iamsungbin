Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have an accredited artistic qualification?

Yes, of course.
It has representative qualifications among the criteria for artists in Korea.

I am receive a valid artist activity certificate from the Korean Artist Welfare Foundation, which is legally recognized. This is the only document certifying an artist by the Korean government, and it is a certificate that is judged and issued according to the strict standards and conditions of the foundation.

The artist-pass associated with it also holds a valid pass.

Do you have a business license?

Yes that's right.
If necessary, tax processing through business registration is possible.

However, in a general project environment, most clients want to contract individually.
Therefore, I generally contract as a freelancer and comply with the lawful tax payment procedures in accordance with Korean laws, such as withholding tax.

How much does the contract cost?

I can't tell you exactly.
Every project is on a different scale.

I negotiate an appropriate amount with the client.
In addition, when working with the government, projects are carried out according to the standards announced by the central government.

What projects are available?

Dramas, movies, advertisements, music videos, documentaries, musicals, performances such as concerts, and exhibitions such as photo exhibitions are all available.

When a large-scale project is required, team project is also possible by forming a team with outstanding experts in the field.
