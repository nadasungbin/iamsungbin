Seoul Arts Video Festival

The 2021 Seoul Arts Video Festival was held safely in the severe situation of the re-spreading of COVID-19 in Korea.

This festival was held for the purpose of suggesting the graduation works of the Broadcasting majors of Seoul Institute of the Arts and as a place of harmony to exchange with seniors who are active in the field.

I participated as a production team and conducted master planning necessary for event budget management.
In addition, he took on the role of the promotion team leader and was sponsored by Sony's camera equipment, designer Lee Sang-bong's costume, and Dong-A Pharmaceutical's Bacchus.

Through this, a lot of effort was put into promoting the sponsor company as well as the main event.

To prevent COVID-19, I have worked closely with local authorities and created events that comply with the quarantine policies of the quarantine authorities.

For more information, please refer to the local media reports related to the festival.
